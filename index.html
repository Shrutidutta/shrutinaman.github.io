Wedding buses from Delhi are the easiest option for most guests. If you're coming from Chandigarh, you can drive or cab directly to the resort.

From Chandigarh

Drive or cab

  • Drive or book a cab directly to Grand View Resort, Jamta.
  • Leave early for a relaxed daytime arrival — the last stretch is through the hills.
  • Save key contact numbers before starting the hill drive, as network can be patchy.

What to pack

Hill station essentials

Jamta is in the Shivalik hills — beautiful but cooler than the plains, especially in the evenings. Here's a quick packing guide.

🧥

Warm layers

Evenings get chilly in the hills. Carry a light jacket or shawl — you'll need it for the sangeet and varmala.

👟

Comfortable shoes

The resort has outdoor spaces and uneven paths. Pack a pair of comfortable shoes alongside your wedding footwear.

👗

Outfit guide

Yellow tones for the haldi, your most glamorous look for the sangeet, and something elegant for the varmala evening.

🕶️

Sun essentials

Daytime events are outdoors — sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat will keep you comfortable through the haldi.

🔋

Power bank

Mobile network can be patchy in the hills. Carry a power bank and download offline maps before the drive.

💊

Personal medication

Carry any prescription medication you need. The nearest pharmacy is in Nahan, about 12 km from the resort.
