Travel

Getting to Jamta

The Delhi bus option is the easiest choice for guests travelling with the family. Guests coming from Chandigarh can travel directly to the venue by personal car or cab.

From Delhi

Wedding buses

  • Departure: 18 April, around 4:00 AM.
  • Bride's side pickup: Mayur Vihar Phase 1.
  • Groom's side pickup: Janakpuri.
  • Please tell us in the RSVP section if you would like to travel by the wedding bus.
From Chandigarh

Drive or cab

  • Guests travelling from Chandigarh can drive or book a cab directly to Grand View Resort.
  • We recommend leaving early enough for a comfortable daytime arrival.
  • Please save your key contact numbers before starting the hill drive.
En route

Delhi → Jamta road guide

The drive is about 280 km on NH-44 and NH-72. Start early, stop for parathas, and enjoy the hills on the last stretch.
